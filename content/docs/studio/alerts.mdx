---
title: Alerts
description: An overview of the Alerts page in the Agentsmith Studio.
---

The User Alerts page displays important notifications related to your account and activity within Agentsmith.

From this page you can:

- View a list of all your alerts, including their date, title, type, and status (`Read` or `Unread`).
- Mark unread alerts as read.
- If an alert is related to a roadmap item, you can click the link to view the roadmap item directly.
