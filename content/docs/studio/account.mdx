title: Account
description: An overview of the Account page in the Agentsmith Studio.
---

The Account page displays your user information and organization memberships.

![Account Page Screenshot](/studio-screenshots/account.png)

From this page you can:

- **Sign Out**: Log out of your Agentsmith account.
- **View Your Details**: See your email address and a link to your GitHub profile.
- **Manage Organization Memberships**: View a list of all organizations you are a member of, along with your role in each. You can also join a new organization from this section if you have an invite code.
